Protx is a cutting-edge tracking and safety app that revolutionizes the way families stay connected and secure in today's digital age. Problem statements

In an increasingly digital world, families struggle to stay connected and ensure the safety of their loved ones, especially when they're apart. There's a need for a reliable and intuitive solution to address this challenge. Protx aims to provide a comprehensive tracking and safety app to help families effortlessly connect and monitor the well-being of their members, regardless of their physical location

Solutions

Protx is a sophisticated tracking and safety app that addresses the challenge of ensuring family safety and connectivity in the digital age. By offering real-time tracking, geofencing, audio capture, and instant alerts, Protx empowers families to stay connected and protect their loved ones. Protx provides a user-friendly interface and customizable features to bridge the gap between family members, enhance safety, and offer peace of mind.
